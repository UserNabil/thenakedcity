We will be delighted to welcome you in our restaurants.

We are two brothers from Buffalo, New York. Along with support from our father, wife, girlfriend, friends and extended family have created Naked City Pizza.

Our family works each day in our restaurants to hand craft as many ingredients as possible. We honor our roots and family upbringing, while we are also trying to create and innovate on a daily basis.
